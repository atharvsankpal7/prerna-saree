1. Category Management
This section allows you to organize your inventory (e.g., Silk, Cotton, Party Wear).

1.1 Category Cover Page: An image upload field to set a featured banner for the category page.

1.2 Category Name: A text field to input the title (e.g., "Prerna Collection").

1.3 Delete Option: A secure "Trash" icon or button to remove categories that are no longer in stock.

2. Product Management
Use this to list specific items like your Navy Blue Zari Work Saree.

2.1 Select Category Button: A dropdown menu linked to your "Add Categories" list.

2.2 Add Product Media: * Primary Photo: The main display image.

Image Gallery: Ability to upload multiple angles (front, back, fabric close-up).

2.3 Product Name: Descriptive title (e.g., "Koskii Navy Blue Soft Silk Saree").

2.4 Product Description: A text area for storytelling and style tips.

2.5 Product Specifications: A structured table or list for details like:

Fabric: Soft Silk.

Work: Zari Embroidery.

Color: Navy Blue.

3. Video Content (Influencer & Dispatch)
Video content builds high trust with customers.

3.1 & 4.1 Video Upload: YouTube/Instagram Reel links.

3.2 & 4.2 Descriptions: Influencer: Mention the creator's name and their review summary.

Dispatch: A "Behind the Scenes" note showing how the saree is packed to ensure quality.

4. Customer Review Management
Moderation Toggle: An "Approve/Reject" button in the Admin Panel so you can filter reviews before they appear on the website.

Rating System: A 1-5 star selection.

Photo Reviews: Allow customers to upload photos of themselves wearing the saree.
